Our Story

From two friends who learned the hard way, welcome to ProfitProphet.

Built by real people who wanted a safer, clearer way to approach crypto.

ProfitProphet started the same way a lot of crypto journeys do: with excitement, curiosity... and a few painful mistakes.

We're just two friends who got pulled into crypto because we believed in the possibilities: financial freedom, new technology, and the chance for regular people to build something meaningful. But like many others, we learned quickly that the space can be overwhelming and chaotic. We lost money trusting the wrong things, and that frustration became the reason ProfitProphet exists.

We didn't want another flashy platform built by a faceless company making promises. We wanted something we would actually use ourselves: a tool that helps everyday people approach crypto more thoughtfully, with better information, smarter testing, and less blind risk.

So we built ProfitProphet as a small independent project, designed by real people who've been on the losing side of bad trades and scams.

Our goal isn't to sell a fantasy of instant wealth. It's to create a practical tool that helps people test strategies, trade safer, and spot better opportunities without jumping in blindly.

We believe crypto should be more accessible and less intimidating which is why we're building ProfitProphet with the long game in mind: a trusted foundation, a real community, and tools that genuinely help people make better decisions in a space that often profits from confusion.

What is included

ProfitProphet ships as a local bot package with a browser control center.

The package is designed for Windows quick start and manual Python launch. It includes setup scripts, a local dashboard, runtime logs, backtest tooling, strategy metadata, and live-mode configuration for Solana execution through supported APIs.

  • Local browser control panel
  • Windows install and start scripts
  • Saved settings import/export
  • Preflight health checks
  • Practice mode for safer testing
  • Live wallet mode for real Solana trades
  • Strategy scorecards and advisor snapshots
  • Operator logs for review and troubleshooting
