Our story

From two friends who learned the hard way, welcome to ProfitProphet.

ProfitProphet started the same way a lot of crypto journeys do: with excitement, curiosity, and a few painful mistakes.

We are two friends who got pulled into crypto because we believed in the possibilities: financial freedom, new technology, and the chance for regular people to build something meaningful. But like many others, we learned quickly that the space can be overwhelming and chaotic.

We lost money trusting the wrong things. That frustration became the reason ProfitProphet exists.

We did not want another flashy platform built by a faceless company making promises. We wanted something we would actually use ourselves: a tool that helps everyday people approach crypto more thoughtfully, with better information, smarter testing, and less blind risk.

So we built ProfitProphet as a small independent project, designed by real people who have been on the losing side of bad trades and scams.

Our goal is not to sell a fantasy of instant wealth. It is to create a practical tool that helps people test strategies, trade safer, and spot better opportunities without jumping in blindly.

We believe crypto should be more accessible and less intimidating. That is why we are building ProfitProphet with the long game in mind: a trusted foundation, a real community, and tools that genuinely help people make better decisions in a space that often profits from confusion.

Built by two friends Independent, practical, and made for everyday traders.

Bot capabilities

Everything follows a clear scan, test, manage, review flow.

ProfitProphet is designed to help you work through trading decisions more carefully. It does not promise winning trades, but it does give you tools to test settings, filter candidates, track outcomes, and reduce emotional clicking.

01

Practice + Paper Modes

Start with simulated trading, then paper trade using live market data before thinking about small supervised live trades.

02

Token Discovery

Use supported Solana sources such as Jupiter, DEX Screener, optional Birdeye-style sources, cached candidates, and watchlists when configured.

03

Entry Filtering

Review liquidity, holder rules, source confidence, quote freshness, price impact, token cooldowns, and clear rejection reasons.

04

Risk Controls

Configure stop loss, take profit, trailing stop, max positions, max trade size, daily loss limits, drawdown limits, and cooldowns.

05

Jupiter Execution Setup

Live-mode setup is designed around Jupiter quote and swap configuration with slippage, price-impact, RPC health, and preflight checks.

06

Dashboard + Telemetry

Use a local browser dashboard to monitor positions, logs, skipped trades, source performance, strategy behavior, equity curve, and runtime events.
